Order & Checkout FAQs

Find quick answers about placing orders, payments, and delivery when buying books through BookTok.

Need help with something specific? Reach out via the contact links in the footer and we will be happy to assist.

Browse the catalog, add books to your order, and then review them on this page. Once you are ready, confirm or update your details and click Place order to complete the checkout.

BookTok is designed to work with modern, secure payment providers. In a production setup this can include major credit and debit cards, digital wallets, and region-specific options depending on your configuration.

Order update and cancellation windows depend on your shipping and fulfillment setup. In a real deployment, you would typically allow edits for a short period before processing begins.

Estimated delivery times depend on your region and the shipping partners you configure. For a typical setup, orders are processed within a couple of business days and delivered shortly after.

For a simple prototype, you can allow guest checkout. In a full implementation, accounts help you track orders, wishlist items, and reading history in one place.
